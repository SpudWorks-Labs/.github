# Support


Welcome to SpudWorks. We appreciate your help in strengthening the backbone of our projects.


### Technical Support & Feedback

For most inquiries, please use our GitHub repositories:

* **Bugs and Feature Requests:** Please open an [Issue] in the respective repository.

* **General Questions:** Open a [Discussion], or check the project Wiki if available.


### Direct Inquiries

For larger concerns, partnership proposals, or matters that require private attention, please contact us directly at: `spudworks.engineering@gmail.com`
