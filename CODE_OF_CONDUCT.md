# SpudWorks Code of Conduct


### Our Pledge

At SpudWorks, we prioritize fostering an environment built on kindness, human decency, and professional integrity.


### Our Standards

We do not accept contributors or enthusiasts who fail to act with basic respect towards others.

Prohibited behavior includes, but is not limited to:

* Harassment or exclusionary behavior.

* Doxxing, insulting, or derogatory comments.

* Personal, political or religious attacks.


### Enforcement

The leadership of SpudWorks (SpudWorks-Labs) is responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior.

We reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
