# Security Policy


### Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability within SpudWorks projects, please do not disclose the information publicly.

Instead, please report it privately to `spudworks.engineering@gmail.com`.


**Please include the following in your report:**

* **Description:** A detailed summary of the vulnerability.

* **Steps to Reproduce:** A clear list of steps to trigger the issue.

* **Environment:** Your OS, dependency versions, and any relevant hardware/software specs.

* **Impact:** How this vulnerability could affect SpudNet or its users.

* **Notes:** Any other extra information that could help patch the vulnerability.


### Our Commitment

We will acknowledge receipt of your report within **48 hours** and provide a timeline for resolution. We appreciate your help in keeping the SpudWorks backbone secure.
